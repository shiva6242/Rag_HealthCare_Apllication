Treatment Protocols

Treatment protocols are standardized medical procedures designed to ensure
consistent and effective patient care.

Diabetes Treatment Protocol:
Diabetes management includes blood glucose monitoring, insulin therapy,
oral antidiabetic medications, dietary modifications, and regular physical activity.
Patients are advised to monitor blood sugar levels daily and attend routine check-ups.

Hypertension Treatment Protocol:
Hypertension is managed through lifestyle changes such as reduced salt intake,
weight management, regular exercise, and antihypertensive medications.
Blood pressure should be monitored frequently.

Respiratory Infections Treatment:
Respiratory infections are treated based on severity. Mild cases require rest
and hydration, while severe cases may require antibiotics or antiviral therapy.

Post-Surgical Care:
Post-surgical patients must be monitored for infection, pain, and wound healing.
Follow-up visits are essential for recovery assessment.

Mental Health Treatment:
Mental health conditions are treated using counseling, therapy sessions,
medications, and patient support programs.
